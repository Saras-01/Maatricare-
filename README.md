**MaatriCare – Digital Maternal Healthcare System**

MaatriCare is a premium AI-powered maternal healthcare management platform developed using Flutter and Django. The system connects Mothers, Doctors, and Hospital Administrators through a secure and intelligent healthcare ecosystem.

**Features**

**Mother Module**
- Pregnancy tracking
- Appointment booking
- Real-time chat with assigned doctor
- Video consultation
- Health vitals monitoring
- AI pregnancy risk alerts
- Medical reports
- Vaccination tracking
- Baby development tracking
- SOS emergency support

**Doctor Module**
- Assigned patient management
- Real-time patient chat
- Appointment management
- AI risk monitoring
- Prescription system
- Medical report review
- NICU/Ward monitoring
- Telemedicine consultation

## Admin Module
- Doctor management
- Mother management
- Delivery workflow management
- Hospital analytics
- Emergency monitoring
- AI global monitoring
- Appointment approvals
- System-wide healthcare monitoring
